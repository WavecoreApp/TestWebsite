Frequently Asked Questions

A quick guide to the most common questions about importing music, using the player, exporting your library, and getting support.

To start building your library, open Library, go to All Songs, then tap the plus button. From there, choose a folder with music or select individual files you want to import.

Mark the files or folder you want to add, then tap Import. Once the import is complete, your music appears in the library and is ready for playback, organization, and editing.

The player is designed to keep listening simple and natural. Open any track from your library to begin playback, then use the player controls to pause, resume, move through the track, or switch to another song.

Wavecore keeps playback close to your library workflow, so you can listen, browse, and manage your music without jumping through unnecessary screens.

Open Settings, go to Library, and tap Export Library. This exports your full library, including files that were updated inside Wavecore.

Export includes edited metadata such as track titles, artwork changes, and other saved tag updates, so the exported library reflects the work you already did inside the app.
