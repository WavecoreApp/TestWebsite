Wavecore Privacy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

We believe your music library is yours alone. Wavecore was intentionally designed to respect your digital boundaries—no unnecessary tracking, no required accounts, just your music.

Local Processing

Wavecore works directly with the audio files and metadata you choose to import. From fetching artwork to reading ID3 tags, everything happens locally on your device. We don't monitor your listening habits or beam your library details to external servers.

How Your Data is Used

Your imported files are used strictly to make the app function beautifully. This includes rendering your library interface, enabling smooth playback, letting you edit tags, and exporting your customized tracks back out.

Data Sharing

Simply put: we don't sell or share your data. Because we don't collect personal analytics or library content, there's nothing for us to hand over to third parties. For the exact, legally required privacy labels, please refer to our official App Store listing.

Questions?

If you have any concerns regarding how Wavecore handles privacy, or just want to chat about app security, feel free to reach out to us at wavecoresupport@gmail.com.
