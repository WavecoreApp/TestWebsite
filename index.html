Navigate to Library > All Songs, and tap the + icon. You can select entire folders or individual audio files from your device. Tap Import, and Wavecore will organize them beautifully.

Because you put time into tagging your music, Wavecore ensures you never lose that effort. Go to Settings > Library > Export Library. We'll export your files with all your custom artwork and metadata changes permanently embedded.

Wavecore handles a wide variety of audio files, playlists, and cue sheets:

  • High-Res & Standard Audio: FLAC, ALAC (M4A), WAV, MP3, AAC, AIFF, AIFC, and CAF.
  • Audiobooks & Podcasts: M4B (including full chapter support).
  • Playlists & Metadata: CUE sheets (imported as virtual tracks), M3U, M3U8, PLS, and XSPF.
